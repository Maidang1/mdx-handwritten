# Close the materialized Scene plan contract

Scene plan V1 is a closed, pure-JSON semantic graph materialized through one synchronous `createScenePlan` Interface: deterministic Annotation recipes and approved proposals share the same finalizer, exact recipe and localization versions, canonical-source identity, Annotation targets, labels, Annotation relationships, Annotation gestures, and bounded Plan provenance. Diagnostics remain outside the plan and every failure returns no plan; fixed implementation-owned limits reject oversized input, while coordinates, executable markup, Visual style, renderer settings, arbitrary extensions, prompts, and provider responses are excluded. Optional AI may produce an approved candidate upstream, but ordinary compilation is pure, deterministic, in-process, and never calls a model.
